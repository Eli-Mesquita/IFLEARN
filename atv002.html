Grammar Review

present simple with 'be'

subject + am / is / are
I am hungry.
She is in a cafe.
You are a student.

present simple with 'be'

subject + am / is / are + not
I'm not hungry.
She isn't in a cafe.
You aren't a student.

present simple with 'be'

(question word +) am / is / are + subject…?
Is he at home?
Why are you hungry?
Where is she from?
