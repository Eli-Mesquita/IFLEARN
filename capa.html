Mystery in London

Six women are dead because of the Whitechapel Killer. Now another woman lies in a London street and there is blood everywhere. She is very hurt. You are the famous detective Mycroft Pound, can you catch the killer before he escapes?

INFORMATION

Oxford Bookworms Library

The Oxford Bookworms Library provides enjoyable reading in English, with a wide range of classic and modern fiction, non-fiction, and plays. It includes original and adapted texts in seven carefully graded stages, which take learners from beginner to advanced level.

About the book

This is an interactive story. You can choose what part to read next. Listen to audio and look at the pictures to make a decision.Follow the numbers and examine the options at the end of each section.


About the author

Helen Brooke was born in the north of England. She has worked in many different jobs, in many different countries, all around the world. She now lives near Oxford in England. She is very interested in interactive reading. She has also written Survive! for the Oxford Bookworms Library.
